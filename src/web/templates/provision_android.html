⚠ Read-Only System — Reboot Required

KP4PRA TNC normally runs in read-only mode to protect the SD card. Pairing and trusting an Android phone writes permanent Bluetooth bond data to storage. After the pairing is saved, the system will automatically reboot. After reboot, the phone will remain paired and the RFCOMM bridge will start automatically.

📱 Provisioning Steps
1
Enable Bluetooth on your Android phone
Open Android Settings → Connected devices → Bluetooth and turn it on.
2
Enable Discoverable and Pairable on KP4PRA TNC
Click the buttons below. KP4PRA TNC will be visible for 180 seconds.
3
Scan for your Android phone
Use the scan below to find your phone. Results are not saved to disk.
4
Pair the Android phone
Select your phone from the scan results above and click Pair, or enter the MAC address manually below. The system will remount storage, save the pairing, and reboot.
Found after scanning above, or in Android Bluetooth settings.
5
System reboots and loads saved configuration
After reboot, confirm the phone appears in Paired Devices on the Dashboard. The RFCOMM bridge restarts automatically.
6
Configure APRSDroid
Open APRSDroid → Menu → Preferences → Connection Preferences.
Set Connection Type: Bluetooth TNC.
Set Bluetooth device: KP4PRA TNC.
Set TNC type: KISS.
Tap Connect. APRSDroid will connect over Bluetooth RFCOMM/SPP.
